OUR VISION

Make every restaurant data point provable.

We envision a world where no operator makes a decision on unverified data. Where every KPI carries a Trust Score. Where AI earns trust through deterministic certification — not marketing claims. We're not building another analytics tool. We're building the trust infrastructure that makes all analytics tools trustworthy.

The industry runs on unverified numbers.

Every restaurant operator today makes decisions on data they can't prove is correct. Processors overcharge. DSPs miscalculate. AI tools hallucinate. We built FohBoh because we believe operators deserve certified truth — and the industry deserves infrastructure, not another dashboard.
